ART WITH A PURPOSE

This design is created by Rahul Kumar, to inspire and connect people with nature.

Hand Poured Candles

Sunriver Nature Center & Observatory (SNCO) has served as a hub for environmental education and nature exploration since 1968. Community residents and visitors alike enjoy quiet walks through SNCO’s campus and around Lake Aspen, often taking in views of stunning landscapes, diverse wildlife, and the star-filled night sky. Commemorate special people in your life with a Gift in Tribute at Sunriver Nature Center & Observatory. Amidst eight acres of beautiful landscape along Lake Aspen, your gift will hold steadfast in a unique setting throughout the seasons. You can support and enrich SNCO’s eight-acre campus by creating a living tribute with the gift of a tree.
